Menudo

Description

I will never not drool when I'm seeing menudo being served on our table. So I will be sharing the ingredients to you guys.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef tripe
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 (15 ounce) cans white hominy
  • Â¼ teaspoon chili powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a 16 quart pot combine the tripe and the onions.
  2. Add water until pot is about 3/4 full.
  3. Cover and cook on low heat for about 2 hours, or until the tripe is tender.
  4. Add the hominy, chili powder, and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cover and cook for another 45 minutes to one hour to incorporate the flavors.
  6. Serve with fresh onions, corn tortillas and lemon if desired.
